Final Project

Planning:

I wanted to create a music box, sized 10cm, 10cm by 15 cm with 15cm being the height,
based on the song “Washing Machine Heart” By Mitski Link: https://youtu.be/-047ko4v05s ,
because I love this song and think the lyrics are very interesting.

https://musescore.com/user/29782823/scores/6825611,
and put them and the durations into this code:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Afr_tw5E-SeRHiyeJ57SgJuiy4At9Zi9K1I_tJn2M0k/edit?usp=sharing


I originally wanted to include a servo motor so that the ‘dirty shoes’ could spin inside the washing machine,
but the code was faulty and thus I had removed it.
The basic input is the button and the output is the sound.


Creation of laser cutting file and 3d printing

Planning:

I used Illustrator and TinkerCAD to prepare my files for laser cutting and 3D printing respectively as I was more familiar with those softwares.
I kept the design simple so that painting them would be easier later.

Process:

I engraved some words such as the song title and the lyrics “do mi ti” on the right and left side respectively.
I tested out my wiring here:
https://www.tinkercad.com/things/c3v1DXAV0QF?sharecode=awd6dULLyJaz7DQA_vhyCv311-UTFpPQtEhzcq7eSE0


Final Execution:

I painted the music box blue and pink using acrylic paint because of the melancholic nature of the song and sweet undertones as Mitski is singing it to her someone who does not love her back.
I used Arduino Uno Maker as it already has a built in buzzer.
